Born and raised in Porto, Portugal, in 1993.
My passion for computer engineering was ignited through my early immersion in computer video games.
I embarked on my programming journey by customizing my hi5 profile with HTML, CSS, and JS, when I was just a young boy, a pivotal moment that not only established my enduring connection with coding but also provided a solid foundation for my programming journey. This profound relationship between programming and gaming continues to captivate me, as I'm intrigued by how code can elegantly solve problems, whether through straightforward or intricate approaches.
My journey in the realm of programming has been diverse, encompassing a range of experiences. I've ventured into the Internet of Things (IoT) landscape while working with a prominent consulting firm. At a small design school, I honed my web development skills, contributing to the enhancement and maintenance of the school's portal. Furthermore, I've had the privilege of developing integrations for a global company, showcasing my ability to navigate complex coding challenges on an international scale. This multifaceted journey has molded me into a dedicated programmer, poised to tackle challenges with inventive solutions while bridging my passion for programming and the world of technology.
